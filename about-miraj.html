About Miraj Patel

Designing since the age of 14, Miraj brings a diverse set of skills, across multiple industries, with companies of all sizes including at a design agency, tech startups, and implementing design change in the enterprise. Mostly recently worked as a Product Designer for Prudential Financial, he has also previously worked alongside the teams at Hertz, Vydia, iSpeech, and Ande & Partners.

As a self-starter and curious learner, Miraj considers himself a Jack of All Trades, and a master of none some, practicing User Experience, User Interface Design and Front-End Development. He enjoys taking a human-centric approach to find a balance between both form and functionality in digital interfaces.

When he's not designing, Miraj loves to travel and indulge himself in the diverse cultures and history the world has to offer. His most recent travel include trips to Spain and South Korea. Outside of traveling, Miraj is also a basketball and football fan and loves to support his teams.
