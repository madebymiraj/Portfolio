About me

Hi, my name is Miraj Patel. I am currently a User Experience Designer for Prudential Financial, where I am playing an exciting role in bridging the gap between design & development and pushing the boundaries for design and technology in the Financial Services industry.

My passion for design allows me to face complex problems and provide meaningful solutions by continuously challenging myself to learn and expand my skills in areas outside of the traditional designer role. I consider myself a Jack of All Trades, and a master of none some, practicing User Experience, User Interface Design and Front-End Development. I take a user-centric approach to find a balance between both form and functionality in digital interfaces.

When I am not designing, I enjoy traveling, watching sports, and grabbing a beer with some friends. Oh, and music to me is what coffee is to most people. Spotify helps me power through the day.
